"Her Vine is more than a vineyard; it is a sanctuary where the rhythm of the seasons dictates the pace of life."
— Elena Marais, Founder

Nestled in the Hemel-en-Aarde Valley, our approach is simple: minimal intervention for maximum expression. We believe that true luxury lies in authenticity—the feel of clay soil in hand, the scent of fynbos in the air, and the complex character of a Syrah aged to perfection.

From our hand-thrown ceramic tasting bowls to our linen-clad tasting room, every texture is chosen to ground you.

Join us for a harvest table lunch, explore our cellar collection, or simply sit amongst the vines and breathe.
